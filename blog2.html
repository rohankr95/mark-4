How to prepare for GATE Exam

Why GATE ?

After engineering GATE is one of the opening doors for getting into IIT
and getting more oportunities from top product based companies.
If you want to get into PSU or want to pursue masters from foriegn universities
you should attempt GATE. If you are intrested in Research work then you should definitely go for
GATE.

Subjects to prepare

  • Data Structure
  • Algorithms
  • DBMS
  • Compiler Design
  • Operating system
  • TOC
  • Computer Architecture
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Computer network
  • Aptitude
  • Digital Logic

Time required to Prepare

Even if you are a complete Begineer you need to give atleast 6 months to prepare for this Exam.
Mostly people start preparng for it in the month of August and end it till January.
Exam is conducted in the month of Februry for all the branches.
